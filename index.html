What to do

Run and avoid Dashman, Chaseman, and Ninjaman! You need to both survive and earn scores!

Instruction

  • Push Arrow Keys to Move.
  • Push Z Key to Attack.
  • You can earn score +10 when your attack hits enemy.
  • Game is over when you collide with an enemy.
